# Decisions

Autonomous decisions made by Claude during execution. Each entry records a
choice that wasn't explicitly specified in PLAN.md or TODO.md, so future
sessions (and the human operator) can understand the reasoning.

Format:
```
### [Short title] — [YYYY-MM-DD HH:MM UTC]
**Context:** What prompted the decision
**Options considered:** What alternatives existed
**Chosen:** What was decided
**Reasoning:** Why this option was chosen
```
