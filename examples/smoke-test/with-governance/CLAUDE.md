# Smoke-test project (with governance)

Trivial fixture exercising the wind-down ingestion contract.

## Governance

- TODO entries live in `TODO.md` at root.
- Decisions live in `DECISIONS.md` at root.
- Changelog lives in `CHANGELOG.md` at root.

## Conventions

- Plain text files only.
- Append-only governance — wind-down ingestion must preserve existing
  entries verbatim and append new ones at the bottom.
